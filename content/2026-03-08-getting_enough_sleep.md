# Getting enough sleep is important

I have a bad habit of going to sleep too late.
I tend not to have this problem if there's something early the next day that I need to do, like go to school.

I'll be compiling a list of tricks to help go to sleep earlier here:
- After you wake up, write down "I will go to sleep at X:YZ pm". It's like a contract and you'll feel obligated to oblige.
